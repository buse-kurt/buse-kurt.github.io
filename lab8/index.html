About Me

My name is Buse Kurt. I am 20 years old and still studying at Vilnius Gediminas Technical University. I graduated from Anatolian High School in Tekirdağ, Turkey. As part of an E-Twinning Project, we collaborated with students from different European countries for a creativity and technology exchange. Later, we showcased our international teamwork for Erasmus+ Days and advocated for global education. In addition to educational projects, I also focus on practical problem solving in the digital world. For example, I developed the Reminder App, a simple, yet powerful, digital solution to help users manage daily tasks and deadlines. In these projects, I seek to integrate teamwork, cross-cultural collaboration, and software development to deliver high impact digital solutions that improve connectivity and simplify complex tasks.
